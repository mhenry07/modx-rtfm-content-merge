Quip's tplCommentOptions Chunk

This is the Chunk displayed with the &tplCommentOptions property on the Quip.Quip snippet. It only is shown to authors of the comment before the removeThreshold has passed, or to moderators of the thread.

Default Value 

[[+allowRemove:notempty=`| <a href="[[+removeUrl]]">[[%quip.remove]]</a>`]]

Available Placeholders

All of the placeholders in the tplComment chunk are available here.
