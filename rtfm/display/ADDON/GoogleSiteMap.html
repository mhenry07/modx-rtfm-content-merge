What is GoogleSiteMap?

GoogleSiteMap is a snippet that will display a Google-customized SiteMap for your site.

Requirements

  • MODx Revolution 2.0.0-rc-3 or later
  • PHP5 or later

History and Info

GoogleSiteMap was written by Shaun McCormick (splittingred) as a snippet to display a Google SiteMap, and first released on June 23rd, 2009.

You can view the roadmap here.

Download

It can be downloaded from within the MODx Revolution manager via [Package Management], or from the MODx Extras Repository, available here: http://modxcms.com/extras/package/550

Development and Bug Reporting

GoogleSiteMap is stored and developed in GitHub, and can be found here: http://github.com/splittingred/GoogleSiteMap

Usage

GoogleSiteMap can be called via the Snippet tags.

Snippets

GoogleSiteMap comes with one snippet:

  • GoogleSiteMap

Examples

Display a Google SiteMap. 

[[!GoogleSiteMap]]
